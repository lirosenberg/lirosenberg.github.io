About Us

The Munch is a food blog that hopes to show you the best and trendiest food around. From SoCal to NYC, we only showcase the most Insta-worthy eats.

We had our start on Instagram, where we originally started as a foodie account. We eventually migrated to our own website where we are today.

We hope you take a look at our blog posts and gain some knowledge about trendy restaurants in your city!
